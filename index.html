14 JANUARY 2026 – 17 JANUARY 2026

At Paradigm Youth Camp, we want young people to know that their identity isn’t determined by the world, but by who they are in Christ: Heirs. Heirs of Him, His Kingdom, and His glory. SC26 is going to be 3 days of encounter, stirring a boldness in the hearts of God’s children to live set apart.

“For his Spirit joins with our spirit to affirm that we are God’s children. And since we are his children, we are his heirs. In fact, together with Christ we are heirs of God’s glory. But if we are to share his glory, we must also share his suffering.”
Romans 8:16-17 NLT
